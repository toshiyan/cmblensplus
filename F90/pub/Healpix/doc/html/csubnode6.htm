ang2vec

Routine to convert the position angles of a point on the sphere into its 3D position vector (x,y,z) with , , .

Location in HEALPix directory tree: src/C/subs/chealpix.c 

FORMAT

void vec2ang(double theta, double phi, double *vector);

ARGUMENTS

name & dimensionality kind in/out description
       
theta double IN colatitude in radians measured southward from north pole (in [0,]).
phi double IN longitude in radians measured eastward (in [0, ]).
vector(3) double OUT three dimensional cartesian position vector (x,y,z). The north pole is (0,0,1)
